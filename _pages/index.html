Why/What

We are going to vist Europa. Europa is one of Jupiters moons. It has an icy surface with water underneath it.

We decided to explore Europa because it has plenty of water and possible unique minerals and with those, it is possible that life exists on europa. And if life does exist on Europa then maybe we could earn years of scientific knowledge off the discovery. Space exploration could be vital to human development and collecting mineral samples could be helpful.

About

Rover Design

The rover has a design that includes an drill, camera, and a smaller aquatic drone

It also has a sample analyzer to check for life

Aquatic Drone

The aquatic drone is small so it can fir in with the ice drill on the rover

It also has a sample collector that can bring samples back to the rover

Ice Drill

The ice drill can drill through the thick ice on Europa

It is designed to fit on the back of the rover.

It can also deploy the smaller aquatic drone

Inspiration

Our inspiration is to get a lot of knowledge of possible things on Europa like unique minerals and possible life. With those things we might increase the scientific knowledge on earth by a lot. And also maybe bring more into the economy by bringing plenty of new minerals in. We could also learn more about space from the minerals. We could learn how radiation effects certain minerals

Solving Problems

The Europa expedition team has solutions to many problems like: the water rover runs out of power, So it activates its turbines, lets water flow through them and the water flowing through it generates electricity. The all of the rovers power sources are a gram of plutonium just like the mars rover curiosity, This lets the rover have virtually unlimited power. Many problems require problem solving to solve, so if any problems happen to occur that the AI can't solve it can change from AI control to Manual, human control (the humans are safely on earth) this can be done with all the tech on the mission (ship, rover, water rover)
